Magisk Module that will turn your Android phone into a OnePlus device by adding:
- Oxygen OS apps (Launcher, Weather);
- OnePlus Widget;

## Requirements
- Android 10
- Magisk 20+

## Installation
1. Flash this module.
2. Reboot.
3. Enjoy!
